About Us

Located at No 1 Shop Opposite Apiapum Daily Market in Obubra, Chukwuemeka Electrical/Electronics & Emmy Best Communications is a leading manufacturer and supplier of electrical and electronic products in Cross River State. We specialize in producing high-quality electrical components, electronic devices, and providing comprehensive telecommunications solutions. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us the go-to destination for individuals, businesses, and contractors seeking reliable electrical and communication products. With years of experience in the industry, we understand the unique needs of Nigerian homes and businesses, delivering products that meet international standards while being perfectly suited to local requirements.
